§ Hopf Algebras and combinatorics (WIP)

Started from algebraic topology in the 40s. In late 70s, Rota figured out that many combinatorial objects have the structure of a Hopf algebra.
A hopf algebra is a vector space HHH over a field KKK. together with KKK linear maps m:A→A⊗Am: A \rightarrow A \otimes Am:A→A⊗A (multiplication), U:A→KU: A \rightarrow KU:A→K (unit), Δ:H→H⊗H\Delta: H \rightarrow H \otimes HΔ:H→H⊗H (comultiplication) S:A→AS: A \rightarrow AS:A→A (co-inverse/antipode).
