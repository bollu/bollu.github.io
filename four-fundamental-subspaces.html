Let AAA be m×nm \times nm×n. The Null space of AAA is in Rn\mathbb R^nRn. The column space is in Rm\mathbb R^mRm. The rows of AAA are in Rn\mathbb R^nRn. The nullspace of ATA^TAT is in Rm\mathbb R^mRm. We want a basis for each of those spaces, and what are their dimensions?
  • The dimension of the column space is the rank rrr.
  • The dimension of the row space is also the rank rrr.
  • The dimension of the nullspace is n−rn - rn−r.
  • Similarly, the left nullspace must be m−rm - rm−r.
