§ Simply connected spaces

  • A space is simply connected iff fundamental group at all points is trivial.
  • We usually don't want to talk about basepoint, so we assume that the spaceis path-connected. This means we can move the basepoint around, or not take about the basepoint.
  • So, a path-connected space is simply connected iff the fundamental group istrivial.
