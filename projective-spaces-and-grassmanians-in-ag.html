§ Projective spaces and grassmanians in AG

§ Projective space

  • Projective space is the space of all lines through Rn\mathbb R^nRn.
  • Algebraically constructed as (V−{0})/R×(V - \{ 0 \})/ \mathbb R^\times(V−{0})/R×.
  • We exclude the origin to remove "degenerate lines", since the subspace spanned by {0}\{0\}{0} when acted on with R×\mathbb R^\timesR×is just {0}\{ 0 \}{0}, which is zero dimensional.
