§ Tensor is right exact

Consider an exact sequence
0→A→fB→gC→0 0 \rightarrow A \xrightarrow{f} B \xrightarrow{g} C \rightarrow 0 0→Af​Bg​C→0
We wish to consider the operation of tensoring with some ring RRR. For a given ring morphism h:P→Qh: P \rightarrow Qh:P→Q this induces a new morphism R⊗h:R⊗A→R⊗BR \otimes h: R \otimes A \rightarrow R \otimes BR⊗h:R⊗A→R⊗B defined by h(r⊗a)≡r⊗h(a)h(r \otimes a) \equiv r \otimes h(a)h(r⊗a)≡r⊗h(a). So we wish to contemplate the sequence:
R⊗A→R⊗fR⊗B→R⊗gR⊗C R \otimes A \xrightarrow{R \otimes f} R \otimes B \xrightarrow{R \otimes g} R \otimes C R⊗AR⊗f​R⊗BR⊗g​R⊗C
To see if it is left exact, right exact, or both. Consider the classic sequence of modules over Z\mathbb ZZ:
