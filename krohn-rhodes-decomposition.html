§ Krohn-Rhodes decomposition

We denote partial functions with X⇀YX \rightharpoonup YX⇀Y and total functions with X→YX \rightarrow YX→Y. A set XXX equipped with a binary operator ⋆:X×X→X\star: X \times X \rightarrow X⋆:X×X→X which is closed and associative is a semigroup.
