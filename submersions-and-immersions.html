Who in the world decided their names? I remember which is which based on the sound. "Submersion" is "surjective", "immersion" is "injective". But really, the naming makes no sense. I can intuitively submerge a ring (a 1D object) into the ocean (a 3D object). so if anything, I'd expect submersions to be locally injective. You can only submerge XXX into YYY if YYY is "larger" than XXX. The definition asks for the precise opposite!
