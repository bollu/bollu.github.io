§ Hook length formula

Truly remarkable formula that tells us the number of standard young tableaux for a given partition λ\lambdaλ of nnn. Recall the definitions:
  • A partition λ\lambdaλ of the number nnn.
  • An assignment of numbers {1,2,…n}\{1, 2, \dots n\}{1,2,…n} onto the diagram of the partition such that the assignment is (a) weakly increasing in the rows, and (b) strictly increasing in the columns. It is strictly increasing in the columns because gravity acts downwards.

§ Knuth's heuristic

  • Consider the hook shape. The only constraint we have is that the top-left number ought to be the smallest.
