  • xxx and yyy coordinates of points on a disk.
  • E[X],E[Y]E[X], E[Y]E[X],E[Y] is zero because symmetric about origin.
  • E[XY]=0E[XY] = 0E[XY]=0 because of symmetry along quadrants.
  • Thus, E[XY]−E[X]E[Y]E[XY] - E[X] E[Y]E[XY]−E[X]E[Y], the covariance, is zero.
  • However, they are clearly correlated. Eg. if x=1x = 1x=1, then yyy must be zero.
