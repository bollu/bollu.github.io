§ Open mapping theorem

  • Given a surjective continuous linear map f:X→Yf: X \to Yf:X→Y, image of open unit ball is open.
  • Immediate corollary: image of open set is open (translate/scale open unit ball around by linearity) to cover any open set with nbhds.

§ Why surjective

  • Consider the embedding f:x↦(x,x)f : x \mapsto (x, x)f:x↦(x,x) from R\mathbb RR to R2\mathbb R^2R2.
  • The full space R\RR is open in the domain of fff, but is not open in R2\mathbb R^2R2, since any epsilon ball around any point in the diagonal (x,x)(x, x)(x,x) would leak out of the diagonal.
  • Thus, not every continuous linear map maps open sets to open sets.
