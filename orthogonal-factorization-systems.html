§ Orthogonal Factorization Systems

  • For a category CCC, a factorization system consists of sets of morphisms (E,M)(E, M)(E,M) such that:
  • E,ME, ME,M contain all isos.
  • E,ME, ME,M are closed under composition.
  • every morphism in CCC can be factored as M∘EM \circ EM∘E
