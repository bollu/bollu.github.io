$y[n] = \frac{1}{N}\sum{m = 0}^{N - 1} x(n - m)$
$y[n] = \frac{x[n]}{N} + \frac{1}{N}\sum{m = 1}^{N - 1} x(n - m)$ + \frac{1}{N}x(n - N) - \frac{1}{N} x(n - N)
$y[n] = y[n - 1] + \frac{1}{N}(x(n) - x(n - N))$
