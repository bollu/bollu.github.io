Let $A$ be the matrix. The $LU$ decomposition is:
\begin{align*}
    &A = LU \\
    &\begin{bmatrix} 
    b_1 & c_1 & &  &\\
    a_2 & b_2 & c_2 & & \\
     & a_3 & b_3 & c_3  & \\
     & &  \ddots & \ddots & \ddots \\
     &&  & a_{n-1} & b_{n-1} & c_{n-1} \\
     &  & & & & a_n & b_n
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\
    l2 & 1 \\
       & l3 & 1 \\
       & & \ddots & \ddots \\
       &    & & l_n & 1
    \end{bmatrix}
 \begin{bmatrix}
v_1 & c_1 \\
    & v_2 & c_2 \\
    & & \ddots & \ddots \\
    & & & v_{n-1} & c_{n-1} \\
    & & & & v_n
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
\begin{align*}
    &b_1 = v_1 \implies v_1 = b_1 \\
    &a_k = l_k v_{k-1} \implies l_k = a_k / v_{k-1} \\
    &b_k = l_k c_{k-1} + v_k \implies v_k = b_k - l_k c_{k-1}
\end{align*}
