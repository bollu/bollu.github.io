§ Exactness of modules is local

We wish to show that for some ring RRR and modules K,L,MK, L, MK,L,M a sequence K→L→MK \rightarrow L \rightarrow MK→L→M is exact iff Km→Lm→MmK_m \rightarrow L_m \rightarrow M_mKm​→Lm​→Mm​ is exact for every maximal ideal m⊂Rm \subset Rm⊂R. This tells us that exactness is local.
