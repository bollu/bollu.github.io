§ Homotopic maps produce same singular homology: Intuition

Take two maps f,g:X→Yf, g: X \rightarrow Yf,g:X→Y which are homotopic. We wish to show that if fff is homotopic to ggg, then we will get the same induced singular homology groups from fff and ggg. Think of the 0D case in a path-connected space, where all points become equivalent since we can connect them by paths.
