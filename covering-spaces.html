§ Path lifting and Monodromy

  • Monodromy is behaviour that's induced in the covering space, on moving in a loop in a base.
  • Etymology: Mono --- single, drome --- running. So running in a single loop /running around a single time.
  • Holonomy is a type of monodromy that occurs due to parallel transport in a loop, to detect curvature
  • Loop on the base is an element of π1(X)\pi_1(X)π1​(X).
  • Pick some point x∈Xx \in Xx∈X. Consider F≡π−1(x)F \equiv \pi^{-1}(x)F≡π−1(x) (FFF for fiber).
  • Now move in a small loop on the base, γ\gammaγ. The local movement will causemovement of the elements of the fiber.
  • Since γ(1)=γ(0)\gamma(1) = \gamma(0)γ(1)=γ(0), the elements of the fiber at the end of the movementare equal to the original set FFF.
  • So moving in a loop induces a permutation of the elements of the fiber FFF.
  • Every element of π1(X)\pi_1(X)π1​(X) induces a permutation of elements of the fiber FFF.
  • This lets us detect non-triviality of π1(X)\pi_1(X)π1​(X). The action of π1(X)\pi_1(X)π1​(X) on the fiberlets us "detect" what π1(X)\pi_1(X)π1​(X) is.
  • We will define what is means to "move the fiber along the path".
