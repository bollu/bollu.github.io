§ Spin groups

  • Spin group is a 2 to 1 cover of SO(n)SO(n)SO(n).
  • We claim that for 3 dimensions, Spin(3)≃SU(2)Spin(3) \simeq SU(2)Spin(3)≃SU(2). So we should have a 2 to 1 homomorphism ρ:SU(2)→SO(3)\rho: SU(2) \to SO(3)ρ:SU(2)→SO(3).
  • We want to write the group in some computational way. Let's use the adjoint action (how the lie group acts on its own lie algebra).
  • What is the lie algebra su(2)su(2)su(2)? It's trace-free hermitian.
