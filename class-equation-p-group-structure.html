§ Centralizer

  • The centralizer of a subset SSS of a group GGG is largest subgroup of GGG which is the center of SSS. § ppp-group

  • A ppp group is a group where every element has order divisible by ppp.
  • Claim: a finite group is a ppp-group iff it has cardinality pNp^NpN for some NNN.
