ยง Polynomial root finding using QR decomposition

  1. For a polynomial p(x)p(x)p(x), build the companion matrix P(x)P(x)P(x).
  2. Show that the characteristic polynomial cp(P)cp(P)cp(P) of the companion matrix P(x)P(x)P(x) is indeed p(x)p(x)p(x).
  3. Find eigenvalues of P(x)P(x)P(x), which will be roots of p(x)p(x)p(x), since the eigenvalues of a matrix MMM are the roots of its characteristic polynomial cp(M)cp(M)cp(M).
  4. We use QR since it is numerically stable. The QQQ matrix discovered by QR is orthogonal, and hence does not disturb the covariance of the noise on matrix multiplication.
