§ Number of irreducible representations

Recall that the characters of irreducible representations are orthogonal. Also, the dimension of the space of class functions is equal to the number of conjugacy classes of the group GGG, since a class function takes on a distinct value over each conjugacy class, so there are those many degrees of freedom. This tells us that the number of irreducible representations is at most the number of conjugacy classes of the group.
